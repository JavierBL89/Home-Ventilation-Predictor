🧠 How the Model Works

The model predicts the most optimal time of the day to ventilate your house based on indoor and outdoor temperatures. However, for real-world application, additional factors like humidity levels and continuous temperature monitoring would be necessary for greater accuracy.

🌍 Prediction Limitations

Due to OpenWeather's free-tier limitations, predictions are made up to 2 days ahead using hourly forecast data (available every 3 hours). Forecasting is constrained to a 5-day window from the time of request.
